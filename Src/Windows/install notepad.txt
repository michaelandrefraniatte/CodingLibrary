To get the name of notepad with PowerShell, enter the command below in PowerShell. Then, press the enter key on your keyboard

Get-WindowsCapability -Online | Where-Object {$_.Name -like "*notepad*"}

The command will display the name as “Microsoft.Windows.Notepad~~~~0.0.1.0”

Add-WindowsCapability -Online -Name Microsoft.Windows.Notepad~~~~0.0.1.0
