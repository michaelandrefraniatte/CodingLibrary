Yes, You can.  
Go to https://photos.google.com/settings and set  to "Sync photos & videos from Google Drive" to "ON";
Then,
Go to https://www.youtube.com/upload and on the right-hand side, you should see an option to import videos from Google Drive;
Click the "import" button, click on your video and choose select.
