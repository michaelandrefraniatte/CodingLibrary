1. Open Windows 10 settings, go to Privacy -> Microphone.
2. Scroll down and in the list of Microsoft Store Apps with access, enable it for "Xbox Game Bar".
3. Exit Xbox Beta completely (can right click on task bar icon and chose "Quit").
4. Run Xbox Beta again.  
Audio settings should now work.
